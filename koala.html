KOALA

The koala (Phascolarctos cinereus) is a tree-dwelling marsupial native to Australia. It is famous for its fluffy ears, large nose, and sleepy demeanor.

Koalas feed exclusively on eucalyptus leaves, which are tough and low in nutrition. As a result, they sleep up to 20 hours a day to conserve energy.
