ANIMALS MAP PLACES

GORILLA

Gorillas (Gorilla gorilla and Gorilla beringei) are the largest living primates, native to the forests of central Africa.

They are highly intelligent and live in family groups led by a dominant male called a silverback.

Gorillas are mainly herbivorous, feeding on leaves, stems, fruit, and shoots.

Despite their size and strength, gorillas are gentle and social creatures, known for their deep bonds and calm nature.
