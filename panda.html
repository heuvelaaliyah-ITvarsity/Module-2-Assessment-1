PANDA

The giant panda (Ailuropoda melanoleuca) is native to China and is known for its distinctive black and white fur and gentle nature.

Pandas primarily eat bamboo, which makes up more than 99% of their diet. They can spend up to 14 hours a day eating!

Despite being classified as carnivores, pandas rarely eat meat. They live in mountain forests and play a crucial role in spreading seeds and maintaining bamboo forests.

Pandas are a symbol of peace and conservation worldwide and are considered a national treasure in China.
